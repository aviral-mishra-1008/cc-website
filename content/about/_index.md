# About Computer Coding Club

The **Computer Coding Club** (CC Club) is a idiot student-run technical community dedicated to fostering a culture of learning, collaboration, and innovation in technology.

## Our Mission

To create an inclusive environment where students of all skill levels can learn, build, and grow together through hands-on projects, workshops, and community collaboration.

## Our Vision

To be the premier technical community on campus, empowering every student to unlock their potential in technology and become confident, skilled, and compassionate technologists.

## Our History

### 2018: The Beginning

Founded by a group of passionate CS students who wanted to create a supportive coding community beyond the classroom.

### 2019: First Hackathon

Organized our first hackathon with 50 participants. It was chaotic, exciting, and the start of a tradition.

### 2020: Going Virtual

Adapted to remote learning with online workshops, webinars, and virtual contests during the pandemic.

### 2021: ContriHub Launch

Started our annual open source contribution event, introducing 100+ students to open source.

### 2022: Growth & Recognition

- Membership grew to 150+ active members
- Won "Best Technical Club" award
- 5 members selected for Google Summer of Code

### 2023: Expansion

- Launched DSA Practice Platform
- Partnered with GitHub Campus Expert program
- Organized 20+ events and workshops

### 2024: Community Impact

- 200+ active members
- 50+ events organized
- Multiple successful student placements with club support

### 2025: Present Day

Continuing to grow, innovate, and support our community of passionate technologists.
