**ContriHub** is our annual month-long celebration of open source contributions. Every October-November, we organize a beginner-friendly event where students make their first (or continued) contributions to open source projects.

> Unlike competitive coding events, ContriHub focuses on **learning, collaboration, and community building** - not rankings or leaderboards.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to know Git/GitHub?**  
A: No! We teach you through our kickoff workshop. Complete beginners welcome.

**Q: What if I'm not good at coding?**  
A: Documentation, testing, design, and content contributions are equally valued!

**Q: How much time commitment is needed?**  
A: Flexible! Some make 2-3 PRs, others 20+. Both are celebrated. Do what works for you.

**Q: Is this only for CS/IT students?**  
A: No! All branches welcome. We need diverse perspectives.

**Q: Will there be mentorship?**  
A: Yes! We pair beginners with experienced contributors.

**Q: Can I contribute to external projects?**  
A: Yes! Though we encourage starting with club repositories.

**Q: Do I need to attend all sessions?**  
A: No. Weekly sessions are optional support - not mandatory.
