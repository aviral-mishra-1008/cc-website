# Competitive Programming

### Practice Platforms

**LeetCode** - [leetcode.com](https://leetcode.com/)  
2000+ problems, company-specific questions, mock interviews.  
*Best for: Interview preparation*

**Codeforces** - [codeforces.com](https://codeforces.com/)  
Regular contests, rating system, editorial solutions.  
*Best for: Competitive programming*

**CodeChef** - [codechef.com](https://www.codechef.com/)  
Monthly contests, learning tracks, peer discussion.  
*Best for: Beginners to advanced*

**AtCoder** - [atcoder.jp](https://atcoder.jp/)  
Japanese platform, high-quality problems.  
*Best for: Advanced algorithmic challenges*

**HackerRank** - [hackerrank.com](https://www.hackerrank.com/)  
Skill certifications, domain-specific tracks.  
*Best for: Structured learning*
